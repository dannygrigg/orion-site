Unit handling

Conveyor systems built for every unit load

Modular, configurable conveyor systems for parcels, totes, cartons, and polybags. Designed for continuous-flow environments with ZLP accumulation and speeds up to 2m/s. Available in custom RAL colours and fully integrated with your existing infrastructure.

Unit handling

Powered Roller Conveyor

The workhorse of any unit handling system. ZLP accumulation control ensures gentle product handling in dense pick zones, while 24VDC and 48VDC motor drive options suit environments from ambient warehouses to temperature-controlled facilities.

  • 24VDC and 48VDC motor drive systems
  • Customisable roller pitch to suit your product profile
  • Compatible with transfer and swivel wheel transfer modules
  • ZLP (Zero Line Pressure) accumulation control
  • Speeds of up to 2m/s
  • Weights of up to 200kg
  • PolyO, PolyV and belt under roller available
  • Custom RAL colour of your choice

Alignment Conveyors

Belt Conveyor

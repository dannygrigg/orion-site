Food Grade Modular Belt Conveyor

Plastic modular belt conveyor systems for direct food product contact applications. Individual belt segments replace without removing the full belt — minimising maintenance downtime and reducing contamination risk during service.

  • 24VDC, 48VDC and 3-phase motor drive systems
  • Customisable modular belt configurations
  • Speeds of up to 2m/s
  • Weights of up to 25kg per metre
  • Plastic modular belt — food-safe direct contact
  • Individual segment replacement — minimal downtime
  • Stainless steel frame, washdown ready
  • Custom RAL colour of your choice

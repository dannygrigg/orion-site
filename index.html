Why Helix

Built for the realities of modern fulfilment

Most sortation systems require building extensions and months of downtime. Helix is engineered to fit around your operation — not the other way around.

Modular by design
Add or reconfigure 300mm cassette modules as your operation grows. Capacity scales with your business — not your building.
15-minute MTTR
Critical components are designed for rapid swap-out. Mean time to repair is 15 minutes — keeping your operation running at peak.
High-throughput sorting
Swivel wheel divert technology delivers up to 20,000+ parcels per hour with 3 sort directions across any parcel size mix.
Compact footprint
No extensions, no planning permission, no disruption to live operations. Helix installs inside your existing building envelope.
Designed & built in the UK
